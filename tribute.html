Tribute to J.K. Rowling

J.K. Rowling is a British author, screenwriter, and philanthropist. She is best known for writing the Harry Potter series, which has won multiple awards and sold more than 500 million copies, becoming the best-selling book series in history. Rowling is also known for her charity work and advocacy for multiple sclerosis and poverty alleviation.

Notable Achievements

  • Author of the Harry Potter series, one of the best-selling book series in history
  • Recipient of numerous awards, including the British Book Awards Children's Book of the Year, Nestle Smarties Book Prize, and Hugo Award
  • Ranked as the world's highest-paid author by Forbes multiple times
  • Philanthropic work includes founding the charity Lumos, which aims to end institutionalization of children worldwide, and supporting multiple sclerosis research and treatment

Quote

"It is our choices, Harry, that show what we truly are, far more than our abilities." - J.K. Rowling, Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
